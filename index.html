About the course

8‑week live cohort · No‑code to practical AI

Bootcamp features

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community and personalized feedback
  • Capstone project coaching & public showcase
  • Shareable certificate and lifetime tutorial access

Grading rubric

  • Online quizzes — 15%
  • Workshop assignments — 35%
  • Attendance & participation — 10% (≥5 workshops)
  • Final project — 40% (passing ≥80%)

Modules

  1. Websites, documents & presentations: LLM basics; generate Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; simple research websites; AI tools for scientific comms.
  2. AI‑automated data analysis: Code Interpreter & Julius; dashboards; dataset processing; automated reports; spreadsheet automations.
  3. GenAI for research & text analysis: literature search (Elicit, LiteRev); classification; sentiment; knowledge maps; summarisation pipelines.
  4. Custom GPTs & chatbots: context windows, RAG; Botpress; knowledge base integration.
  5. AI for task automation: email flows; agents; browser control for web tasks.
  6. Communication & outreach: summaries for different audiences; figures; video abstracts; visualization prompts; content scheduling.
  7. Capstone & showcase: progress checks; peer review; final presentation & Q&A.
